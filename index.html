About me
Growing up on the shores of the Léman in Switzerland then in the streets of Paris, I've always considered Computer Science as a career path. I still find marvelous the fact that you can build nearly anything with enough dedication. Studying at ISEP, I chose Software Development as my major, as it is undoubtedly the path where I can express at most my creativity. I'm more on the engineering than the designer side, so I prefer simple but effective products. Even though I don't mind when it's also nice to the eyes.
